Frequently asked questions about our habit tracking site

How to register on the site?

Registration on our website is very simple. You need to specify your email, password and confirm your email address.

Is it possible to track multiple habits at once?

Yes, you can add and track as many habits as you like.

What data is stored on the server?

We only store the data you provide. This includes the names of the habits and the time you perform them. We do not collect or store any personal information.

Can I share my habits with other users?

There is currently no habit sharing feature available.
